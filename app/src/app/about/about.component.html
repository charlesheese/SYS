About Us

Welcome to the Northeastern Ticket Resale Hub – a platform created by Northeastern students, for Northeastern students. Founded by Charlie, John, Ripan, and Aryaman, our goal is to make ticket reselling safe, simple, and accessible for the entire Northeastern community.

As students ourselves, we understand the hassle of finding trustworthy sources for concert, event, and sports tickets, or selling unused tickets without excessive fees or scams. Our platform provides a streamlined marketplace exclusively for Northeastern students, offering a safe space for verified buyers and sellers to connect directly.

Whether you’re looking to buy a last-minute ticket to an event or need to sell one, the Northeastern Ticket Resale Hub is your one-stop solution. Join our growing community and let us take the stress out of ticket exchanges so you can focus on enjoying the experience!
